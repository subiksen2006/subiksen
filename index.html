About Me

I create beautiful, functional, and user-centered digital experiences that drive results.

I'm Subiksen, a passionate Full Stack Developer and UI/UX Designer with 5+ years of experience creating digital experiences that users love. My journey in tech began with a fascination for how things work, which evolved into a career where I combine analytical thinking with creative design.

As a developer, I specialize in JavaScript technologies including React, Node.js, and modern frontend frameworks. I believe in writing clean, efficient, and maintainable code that stands the test of time. On the design side, I focus on creating intuitive interfaces that delight users while meeting business objectives.

What sets me apart is my ability to bridge the gap between design and development. I understand both worlds, which allows me to create seamless experiences from concept to implementation. I'm equally comfortable discussing color theory as I am optimizing database queries.

When I'm not coding or designing, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or creating digital art. I'm always excited to collaborate on interesting projects that challenge me to grow.
